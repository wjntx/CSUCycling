Welcome to the offical Website of the CSU Rams Cycling Team...

Whether you are a seasoned pro or have never raced before, we encourage you to join the Rams Cycling Team! We compete in mountain, road, track, cyclocross, and now BMX! Please check the schedule for the current meeting time and location. See our Frequently Asked Questions for more information.

Our team holds events throughout the year for all disciplines so be sure to stay up to date in our google group and watch for how you to get involved. We not only love to race and hold events for our club, but giving back to the cycling community of fort collins is one of our biggest priorities. Holding silent auctions, fundraisers, and other events to help get the local community to come together is something we emphisize through our organization.

Mountain Season

The racing season for mountain biking starts in the beginning of the fall semseter and runs about 8-10 weeks. We race in both cross country and downhill every weekend during the season. Whether you have never raced before and are interested or you have a lot of experince racing, we have a great community that will help push and develop you as a rider.

Road Season

The road season for road biking starts in the middle of the spring semseter and runs about 8-10 weeks. We race in events such as time trials and criteriums. Every year we host a race weekend where we host a criterium around the oval and a race out at cobb lake. Whether you have never raced before and are interested or you have a lot of experince racing, we have a great community that will help push and develop you as a rider.
